About PM Surya Ghar Yojana

  • The Mission: Free Electricity for 1 Crore Households
    PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana is a national initiative promoting rooftop solar installations.
  • The government plans to invest ₹75,000 crore to supply 1 crore households with up to 300 units of free electricity each month.
  • A large-scale scheme like this requires a reliable and low-cost verification system to maintain transparency and public trust.
  • Current challenge: Traditional field inspections are slow, costly, and difficult to scale.
